Hello, I recently graduated with Cum Laude in mechanical engineering from Cal Poly Pomona. My interests are in robotics, smart technology, and manufacturing.

I've done internships overseas with DeltaLight Asia manufacturing light fixtures and with the Chinese University of Hong Kong researching precision micro-contact printing for roll-to-roll fabrication of flexible electronic devices.

I was also involved on campus with the Northrop Grumman Collaboration Project (NGCP) - Unmanned Underwater Vehicle, Human Powered Vehicle Club (HPVC), Pi Tau Sigma Honor Society, Tau beta Pi Honor Society and Epic Movement for which I have held several leadership positions.
